About Golden Wind

Golden Wind was founded in 2020. Our team of experts has over 10 years of experience in handling all types of infestations. We first came up with the idea after we were laid off by the company we were working for because of COVID-19. Instead of wasting our time, the founder, John Smith, invited six other former employees to start a business together with him, and our company has been growing nonstop since.
